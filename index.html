Welcome to TJHSST Machine Learning!

Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology is a magnet school in Alexandria, Virginia. We are known for our science and technology courses. For computer science, TJHSST offers courses in Foundations Java and Python, AP CS A+, Artificial Intelligence, Parallel Computing, Mobile and Web Application devlopment, and Computer Vision. One thing they don't offer is Machine Learning, and we are here to help TJ Students learn to make smarter programs.

What We Do

TJHSST Machine Learning Club aims to bring the complex and vast topic of machine learning to high school students. We teach a variety of topics, including Neural Networks, Convolutional Neural Networks, SVM's, GPU Acceleration, and more.

Competitions and Challenges

We hold challenges every week to supplement the topics we cover in the lectures. Sometimes, we have larger competitions, where everyone competes to make the best algorithm. TJHSST Machine Learning strongly encouurages students to take part in external competitions to improve their knowledge.
